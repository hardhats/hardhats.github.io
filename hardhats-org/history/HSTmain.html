History of VISTA (DHCP)

The history of VISTA (DHCP) is a fascinating topic for several reasons.

  • For some DVA employees, VISTA has become a career long effort and represents a commitment that goes beyond duty.

  • Knowing the history provides reference points to better understand VISTA's current design and philosophy.

  • Plain and simple curiosity. (For instance, why is this site called hardhats.org?)
