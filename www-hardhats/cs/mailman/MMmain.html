 Overview
This page introduces Mailman related information and enhancements.  Mailman is an integrated data channel in Vista for the distribution of:
  • Patches (KIDS builds)
  • Software releases (KIDS builds)
  • Computer-to-computer communications (HL7 transfers, Servers, etc.)
  • Person-to-person messaging (Email)

What features do you need in order for the Mailman to better meet your needs? Have you created enhancements for Mailman that you would like to share with other Hardhats?   Please feel free to tell us. Your feedback is greatly appreciated.
