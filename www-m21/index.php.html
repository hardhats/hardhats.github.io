Welcome to M21 - a company providing an implementation of the M language/database for the 21st Century.

If you're already an M user then we hope you will identify with our mission statement and welcome this development in M technology.

If you're new to M then we think that you are going to be very pleasantly surprised. M is one of the best-kept secrets in the history of computing. Read the New to M section and wonder how such a powerful language and database has been so widely used for so long without a public profile.

Whoever you are, let's hope this is the start of a long-term experience taking our companies and M technology into a new era.
